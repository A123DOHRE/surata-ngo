About Us

We Wanted to make a self Sustainable Surata Village Provide skillful self learning platform to the students

We provide great services and ideass

The Hunarshala is an unschool selfdesigned learning space for students of Govetrement Senior Secandry School Surata Dungarpur Rajasthan.It is being supported by Shikshantar and Ds Group.the learners arefree to decide what they want to explore. There is no centralizedn curriculum rather,each learner is involved in designing their own personalized learning goals.We always start by asking them what they want to learn, how they want to learn, and from whom they wantto learn. There are computer Hub,Stitching Hub,Maker Space,photography,sport and other activity.hunarshala gives children an opportunity to first understand themselves,their uniqueness,their talant and their dreams. It is nurturing them to express themselves without any hesitation and then supporting them in pursuing their intersts and their leadership skills.People look at this as an alternative space,but this is not an alternayive space.it is the need of every child.It is what the mainstream should be.

Computer Technology Hub

After school Students come and learn computer technology in computer we are doing project based learning first we learn some theory without any delay we are doing some practical things related to this theory mainly in computer we learn (Coreldraw ,Photoshope,E-mitra work,Html/Css,Bootrape ,M-S Office(m-s word,m-s publisher,power point,M-S Excel

Stitching Hub

we trained tribal woman and girls in Stitching.we stitch designer blouses,wrapper paint and shirt,diffrent types of bags,diffrent type of salvar kurta. we get order from diffrent places like swaravoice of woman and other places.we need more group who connect with us and work with us.

photography Hub

In photography Students cliq the photos from camera and learn about diffrent technique that are used in camera.Also going for photowalk for cliquking the nature photos. some resouces person are reguarly visit and give some suggetion and tips about photography that give a good support to our children to learn better thing.

Maker's space Hub

we work with young youth and make something new from waste material.we also made diffrent diffrent items using Bamboos.students also learn about elctric house wiring and electric wiring.we also get some order of making new things using Bamboos and we also need more group to connect with us and work with us

Sport Hub

In Sports Hub students play Frisbee and doing skating.we don't have a proper ground for those games but children plying ultimate game and doing a wonderful skating . In Hunarshala childrens are come and play whatever they like most. along this in sport hub children play carrom board and chess.
